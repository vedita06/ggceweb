District level Inter College Women’s Football Competition

District level Inter College Women’s Football Competition* was organized on *2nd November* in the courtyard of Gyan Ganga College of Excellence. In the competition, a football match was organized between *UTD* including the team of *St. Aloysius and Gyan Ganga College of Excellence*, *St. Aloysius College women’s football team won* and *UTD team was runner up*. The team of Gyan Ganga College of Excellence performed well. The students of Panagar Government College also gave their presence in the football competition. Chief guest of the program, Mr. Vishal Banne ji, Director and Head of Physical Education, RDVV, while addressing the students, praised the sports premises of GGCE and the district and state in future. The consent to organize competitions at the level was also taken from the principal. * Shri Awadhesh Kumar Dubey Government College (Panagar), Su Shri Rohila Pillai Ji (St. Aloysius) and Shri Sanjay Kumar Ji from Nachiketa College gave their commendable presence in the competition. * Secretary of Gyan Ganga Group, Dr. Rajneet Jain ji, Shri Pankaj Goyal ji, Shri Apoorva Singhai, Engineer Vishesh Jain ji, encouraged the students by giving their presence. Gyan Ganga College’s Principal Dr. Kirti Vishwakarma addressed the students with these words, accepting the presidency of District Level Inter College Women’s Football Competition 2022-23, it is necessary to participate in daily life for good health. The whole courtyard was drenched in the presence of the students studying in the college. The work of all the staff including all the departmental heads of the college, Lt Geete, Dr. Sativeer Kaur, Dr. Garima Choubey and sports in-charge, Mr. Vikas Pandey made the organization of the competition successful.
