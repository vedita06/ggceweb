BA-LLB | Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Legislative Law

Degree: UG integrated | BA-LLB (Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Legislative law) | Duration: 5 Years | Eligibility: 10+2 with 55%

Welcome to the UG Integrated BA-LLB (Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Legislative Law) program at Gyan Ganga College of Excellence (GGCE). Our five-year undergraduate program is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in both arts and law, preparing them for a rewarding career in the legal field.

Course Overview:

The UG Integrated BA-LLB program at GGCE offers a comprehensive curriculum that combines the study of humanities and law. The program aims to develop critical thinking, analytical skills, legal knowledge, and ethical understanding among students. It covers subjects such as constitutional law, criminal law, contract law, international law, legal research, legal writing, and more.

Duration and Eligibility:

The BA-LLB program has a duration of five years, integrating both the Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Legislative Law degrees. To be eligible for this program, applicants must have completed their 10+2 education from a recognized board with a minimum of 55% aggregate marks. We welcome students who are passionate about law and have a keen interest in social sciences and humanities.

Highlights:

1. Integrated Approach: Our program offers a unique integrated approach that combines the study of arts and law. This interdisciplinary approach provides students with a broader understanding of society, politics, history, and culture, enabling them to approach legal issues from a well-rounded perspective. 2. Expert Faculty: GGCE has a team of highly qualified faculty members with vast experience in the legal profession. They bring their expertise and practical knowledge into the classroom, delivering engaging lectures and fostering a stimulating learning environment. 3. Moot Court Sessions: To enhance practical skills and courtroom experience, we conduct moot court sessions where students simulate legal proceedings. These sessions help students develop skills in legal argumentation, research, and persuasive communication. 4. Internships and Workshops: We emphasize practical learning through internships and workshops. Students have the opportunity to intern with reputed law firms, corporate legal departments, and NGOs to gain hands-on experience and apply their theoretical knowledge in real-world scenarios.

Placement Opportunities:

GGCE is committed to providing strong placement opportunities for our BA-LLB graduates. We have collaborations with prestigious law firms, corporate organizations, government agencies, and judicial bodies. Our dedicated placement cell assists students in securing internships and job placements, conducts mock interviews, and provides career guidance to help students kick-start their legal careers.

Career Opportunities:

Graduates of the BA-LLB program have a wide range of career options available to them. They can pursue careers as lawyers, legal advisors, legal researchers, corporate counsels, legal consultants, and public prosecutors. They can also explore opportunities in litigation, judiciary, civil services, academia, and legal journalism.
