BASIC INFORMATION

Degree: UG | Bachelor of Journalism & Communication (B.J.C) | Duration: 1 Year | Eligibility: Graduation from any field (UG Degree) | Affiliated by RDVV Jabalpur

Welcome to the Bachelor of Journalism & Communication (B.J.C) course at Gyan Ganga College of Excellence (GGCE.) Our one-year undergraduate program is designed to equip students with the essential skills and knowledge required for a successful career in the field of journalism and communication.

Course Overview:

The Bachelor of Journalism & Communication program is a comprehensive and dynamic course that covers various aspects of journalism, media studies, and communication. It provides students with a solid foundation in media ethics, news reporting, writing, editing, interviewing techniques, digital media, and public relations.

Duration and Eligibility:

The course has a duration of one year, allowing students to fast-track their education and enter the workforce sooner. To be eligible for the B.J.C program, applicants must have completed their graduation from any field (UG degree). We welcome students from diverse academic backgrounds who have a passion for journalism and communication.

Course Highlights:

1. Practical Training: Our curriculum emphasizes practical training, ensuring that students gain hands-on experience in various journalistic activities. Through assignments, workshops, and internships, students develop their skills in news writing, video production, photojournalism, and more. 2. Experienced Faculty: GGCE boasts a team of highly experienced and knowledgeable faculty members who have extensive industry expertise. They provide guidance and mentorship to students, helping them navigate the ever-evolving landscape of journalism and communication. 3. State-of-the-Art Facilities: Our college is equipped with modern infrastructure and cutting-edge facilities to support students’ learning. From well-equipped media labs to multimedia studios, students have access to resources that enable them to explore different forms of media production. 4. Industry-Relevant Curriculum: The curriculum is regularly updated to reflect the latest trends and developments in the field of journalism and communication. We emphasize the integration of digital media tools and techniques to ensure our graduates are well-prepared for the digital age.

Placement Opportunities:

At GGCE, we understand the importance of providing our students with strong placement opportunities. We have established relationships with leading media organizations, news agencies, broadcasting companies, and public relations firms. Our dedicated placement cell assists students in securing internships and job placements, connecting them with industry professionals and helping them build a strong network.

Career Opportunities:

A Bachelor of Journalism & Communication degree opens up a wide range of career opportunities in various sectors, including print journalism, broadcast journalism, online media, advertising, public relations, corporate communication, content creation, and more. Graduates can pursue roles such as journalists, news anchors, editors, content writers, media researchers, public relations executives, and social media managers. Join GGCE’s Bachelor of Journalism & Communication program to embark on an exciting journey towards a rewarding career in the world of media and communication. Apply now and become part of our vibrant and dynamic learning community.
