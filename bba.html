Bachelor of Business in Administration (BBA)

Course : Bachelor of Business in Administration (BBA) Degree: Undergraduate (UG) Degree from RDVV, Jabalpur Duration : 3 Years Course Design : Interdisciplinary Intake : 300

Welcome to the UG Integrated BBA(Bachelor of Business in Administration) program at Gyan Ganga College of Excellence (GGCE).

Eligibility Criteria:

10+2 with minimum 55%/UGAT score ; Direct admission on the basis of 10+2 score

Scholarships:

Merit scholarships & State Government Scholarship for eligible students

Programme Outcomes:

100 % Internship Opportunities Computer and Communications Trainings Financial Literacy Trainings Bachelor’s degree in business Administration enhances & instils an undergraduate candidate with high level of professionalism to transform the students into competitive business leaders. The best college to offer BBA Course in Jabalpur, and best amongst in MP. In today’s global business environment, it is a requisite for any candidate to develop the skills in order to overcome the barriers of upcoming career prospects and to encourage the youth for grooming their personality as talented individuals. BBA course contributes towards the aim of fulfilling the needs of MNC’s i.e. corporate sector and government sector as well.
