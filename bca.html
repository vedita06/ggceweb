Bachelors’ in Computer Applications (BCA)

Join BCA at Gyan Ganga Group of Institutions

Welcome to the UG Integrated BA-LLB (Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Legislative Law) program at Gyan Ganga College of Excellence (GGCE). Our five-year undergraduate program is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in both arts and law, preparing them for a rewarding career in the legal field.

BCA Programme Objectives

The basic objective of the programme is to open a channel of admission for computing courses for students, who have done the 10+2 and are interested in taking computing/IT as a career. After acquiring the Bachelor ‘s Degree (BCA) from GGCE/RDVV, there is further educational opportunity to go for an MCA at GGCE or Master ‘s Programme at any other University/Institute if desired. Also, after completing BCA Programme, a student should be able to get entry level job in the field of Information Technology or ITES.

Duration of the Programme

Minimum: 3 Years, Maximum: 6 Years.

Medium of Instruction

The medium of instruction is English. The course material is also in English. However, necessary support will be provided to students coming from vernacular language medium during high school studies.

Credit System

The College follows a unique ‘Credit System’ for its programmes. Each credit is worth 30 hours of student study time, comprising all the learning activities. Thus, a three-credit course involves 90 study hours. This helps the student to understand the academic effort one has to put into successfully completing a course. Completion of the programme requires timely submission of both assignments and the Term End Examination of each course in the programme.

BCA Programme Structure

The programme has been spread over three years. Consequently, there will be one half yearly (internal) one final (annual) university examination every year. The students must appear for both the examinations as per academic calendar/schedule announced by the College/ University. Student may ensure that she/he paid the requisite fee as well as fulfils other requirements such as prescribed minimum attendance etc. before appearing in the term end examinations.
